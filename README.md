#react-native-plaid-login

A login template for Plaid's supported banks. All logins and security questions route to the Plaid sandbox. 

**Build is currently framed. But not functional**

##Supported Banks
* American Express
* Bank of America
* Capital One 360
* Charles Schwab
* Chase
* Citi
* Fidelity
* PNC
* Silicon Valley Bank (beta)
* US Bank
* USAA
* Well Fargo
